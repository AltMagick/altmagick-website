How it works?

It's so simple!

Upload an image

With the AltMagick extension, all you have to do is right-click and click on AltMagick in the menu to send the image to our servers.

Our AI does its ✨ magic ✨

Our AI analyses your image and generates a short, clear transcription of its content. It only takes a few seconds.

Your alt text is ready

Paste it where you want. Whether it's for your website (WordPress, Webflow, etc.), a post on your social networks or in your code editor!

Credits are only valid for one month, and your usage counter is reset every month.

When you cancel your subscription plan, automatic renewals will be turned off and you will not be charged going forward.
You will continue to have access to your current credits until the scheduled renewal date.

Each image costs 1 credit.
We only charge for successfully generated alt text.
If we are unable to generate an alt text for an image, you will not be charged.
If the alt text is in your browser's cache (image with alt text already generated) you will also not be charged.

We do not save your images or alt text generated on our servers.

AltMagick supports a wide range of formats such as JPG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC, HEIF, ...
If AltMagick does not support the format, an automatic conversion will be attempted.
